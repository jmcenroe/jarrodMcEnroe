I'm the developer for your next project!

I haven't always been a developer, but I have always had a curious mind, constantly wondering about the inner workings of things and why they work the way they do. The more I coded, the more I loved it and knew I wanted to become a web developer.

Before that, I worked in regular office jobs for 10 years. The need to get creative and understand the technology I use daily became too strong to ignore. I decided I needed a change. I started by recreating simple mockups of web pages I found. The more I practiced coding as a hobby, the more fun (and frustrating) it became. After many attempts to teach myself to code, I decided to enroll in a coding bootcamp.

There, I have learned many technologies necessary for full stack web development. With these skills, I am able to put both thoughts and functionality together in a way that makes intuitive sense for users and for other developers alike.
